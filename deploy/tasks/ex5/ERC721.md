## Compile contract

Activate virtual Environment

`source ~/cairo_venv/bin/activate`

Compile contract

`starknet-compile contracts/tasks/ex5/ERC721.cairo --output build/ex5/ERC721.json --abi abis/ex5/ERC721.json`
